Design Challenge

#MakeUmuziBetter for the tech recruits that struggle with time management

My team and I conducted user interviews and insights, this was the The first part of the empathize journey was to conduct interviews. 11 Umuzi recruits and staff were interviewed. The questions we posed gave us an understanding on the environment and overall experience of working at Umuzi.

The insight that we got from the recruits and staff is that the main areas of focus revolved around self-learning, environment, culture, umuzi staff & recruits, work, resources and the application process.This is the user journey of Jimmy (male; 25 years old; from Alexander, Johannesburg). Jimmy is in the tech department at Umuzi and his journey shows how he struggles to manage time between Umuzi & Gnomio assignments, leisure activities and self learning. The frames also highlights what the user is doing, thinking and feeling throughout his journey.

We looked into the moments of fuel/friction,moment of truth,insight statements,brainstorming,10 star experiences, validations and testing.

We then choice our minimum viable product which was conducting workshops.
